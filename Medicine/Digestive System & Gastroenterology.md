## Criteria for Liver Transplantation in Hepatocellular Carcinoma (HCC)

### Milan Criteria

- Resectable tumor(s):
  - 1 tumor: < 5 cm
  - ≤ 3 tumors: each < 3 cm
- No vascular invasion
- No extrahepatic metastasis

### UCSF Criteria

- Resectable tumor(s):
  - 1 tumor: < 6.5 cm
  - ≤ 3 tumors: each < 4.5 cm & total < 8 cm
- No vascular invasion
- No extrahepatic metastasis
