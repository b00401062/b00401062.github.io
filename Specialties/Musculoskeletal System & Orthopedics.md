## Indications for Bisphosphonate Therapy

- Osteopenia w/
  - History of fracture
  - 10-year risk for
    - Major osteoporotic fracture > 20%
    - Hip fracture > 3%
- Osteoporosis
- Paget disease
- Multiple myeloma
- Bone metastasis
